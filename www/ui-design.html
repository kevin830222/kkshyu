Resolute Innovation

I contracted with Resolute Innovation in order to create several mockups for their research and development platform. It's a UI study for mobile and desktop to explore a clean, minimal look on sidebars, hidden dashboard and search results screens. Search filters are accessed through a dropdown menu. It is inspired by flat design and contains some elements of material design. Photoshop was the only tool used.
